Set output to **portrait, 4:5 ratio, high resolution**.

`mix-blend-mode: lighten` so the subject needs to read as bright pixels against dark.

## Tips for Nano Banana

- Set **Aspect Ratio** to 4:5 or 9:16 before generating
- If the background isn't dark enough, add: *"near-black background, dark studio"*  
- For better blend-mode results, add: *"extreme contrast between lit subject and dark surroundings"*
- Regenerate 2–3 times and pick the one with the most dramatic shadow separation
- After generating, you can edit in Nano Banana: *"Make the background darker and increase contrast"*
